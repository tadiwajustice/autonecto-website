About Autonecto Electrics

Add more life to your vehicle.

Who We Are

Autonecto Electrics is a mobile auto-electrical and diagnostics company specializing in computerized vehicle diagnostics, hybrid system checks, and on-site electrical repairs. We bring advanced automotive technology solutions directly to our clients, ensuring fast, professional, and reliable service wherever they are.

Our Mission

To provide professional, technology-driven auto-electrical and diagnostic services that enhance vehicle performance, reliability, and safety.

Our Vision

To build a network of highly trained mobile technicians across the region, offering advanced diagnostics and maintenance services for both conventional and hybrid vehicles.

Core Values

  • Precision First: Data-driven diagnostics for accuracy.
  • Integrity & Trust: Honest feedback without unnecessary repairs.
  • Innovation: Adapting to modern vehicle electronics.
  • Commitment to Safety: Prevention-focused service.
  • Partnership: Supporting individuals & businesses.
  • Professionalism: Certified experts committed to excellence.
  • Customer Empowerment: Clear explanations, smarter decisions.
  • Mobility & Accessibility: Service at your home or office.

Services We Offer

  • Computerized Vehicle Diagnostics: Full system scans, fault code analysis.
  • Hybrid & EV Diagnostics: High-voltage system checks.
  • Mobile Auto Electrical Repairs: Wiring, alternators, starters.
  • GPS Tracking & Security: Trackers, alarms, immobilizers.
  • Preventive Maintenance: Battery testing, vehicle health checks.
  • Fleet Services: Depot visits, truck diagnostics, electrical maintenance.
